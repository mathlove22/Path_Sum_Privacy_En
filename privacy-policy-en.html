Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 22, 2024

Path Sum ("Company" or "Service") takes user privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the Path Sum application (the "App") and how that information is used.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, the following information may be automatically collected:

  • Advertising ID: Collected by Google AdMob for personalized advertising.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, screen resolution, and other technical information
  • App Usage Information: Game play statistics, session duration, etc.

1.2 Locally Stored Information

The following information is stored only on your device and is never sent to our servers:

  • High scores
  • Statistics (games played, max combo, etc.)
  • Settings (sound, vibration preferences)
  • Daily revive usage (for daily limit management)

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

Path Sum never collects the following information:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Photos or media files
  • Any other sensitive personal information

2. How We Use Information

Collected information is used only for the following purposes:

  • Game Functionality: Game progress, score tracking, statistics management
  • Advertising: Display personalized ads and measure ad performance
  • Service Improvement: App performance optimization and bug fixes
  • Revive System Management: Enforce daily revive limits

3. Third-Party Services

Path Sum uses the following third-party services:

3.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers to provide personalized ads.

  • AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Opt-out of personalized ads: Device Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

4. Data Protection

We protect your information through the following measures:

  • We do not collect sensitive personal information
  • All statistical data is stored locally on your device only
  • Communications with third-party services (AdMob) are encrypted
  • We do not store user data on our servers

5. Children's Privacy

Path Sum is not directed to children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Data Deletion: All local data is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Opt-out of Personalized Ads: You can disable ad personalization in your device settings
  • Stop Using the App: You can stop using the app at any time

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • All game data is stored locally on your device
  • All data is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Data collected by AdMob is managed according to Google's Privacy Policy

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in legal requirements or service policies. We will notify you of any changes through in-app notices or update notes. For significant changes, we will provide at least 7 days' advance notice.

9. Legal Basis

This Privacy Policy is prepared in accordance with:

  • Personal Information Protection Act (South Korea)
  • Information and Communications Network Act
  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation, where applicable)

10. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Path Sum is a native mobile app and does not use traditional browser cookies. However, we use local storage to save game settings and statistics on your device.
